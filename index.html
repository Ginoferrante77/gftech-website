About GFTECH

GFTECH is a Web3 project built on human, technological, and ethical values. Our mission is to create a transparent and accessible ecosystem that puts technology at the service of the greater good.

The GFTECH token, deployed on the Polygon blockchain, is designed to support real-world initiatives in the following areas:

  • Environmental sustainability and green development
  • Technological and digital education
  • Combating poverty and social isolation
  • Funding humanitarian foundations across the globe

We believe blockchain can be a driver of positive innovation and real-world impact. GFTECH aims to connect people, support meaningful projects, and provide a new form of inclusive economic participation.

Founder

Gino Ferrante is the founder of the GFTECH project. An entrepreneur driven by innovation, ethical technology, and social impact, he created GFTECH as a response to the need for a more human-centered financial model.

With a background in project management, accounting, and Web3 development, Gino brings his skills to build a token model based on solidarity, transparency, and inclusion.
