Our Vision

GFTECH was founded by Gino Ferrante with a single idea: technology should serve humanity. Through blockchain transparency, GFTECH connects compassion and innovation to fund causes that matter — from helping children and families to supporting mental health and poverty relief worldwide.

Message from the Founder

“I believe technology can unite people and bring hope to those in need.”

— Gino Ferrante, Founder of GFTECH

Foundations We Support

GFTECH dedicates 50% of new tokens and contributions to real-world humanitarian foundations helping children, mental health, and global poverty. The remaining 50% fuels liquidity, development, and growth of our community-driven ecosystem.
